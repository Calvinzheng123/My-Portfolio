Champions League Prediction Model

Built a logistic multi-classification model using five years of Champions League data for each team's league performance. Adjusted metrics like points, expected goals (xG), and goal difference based on FIFA coefficient values to account for league strength disparities. Incorporated historical progression data for teams through Champions League stages as features, and assigned weights to each stage to enhance model accuracy in predicting advancement chances.".
